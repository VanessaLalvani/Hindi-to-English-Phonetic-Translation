Dependent
Vowels

Dependent vowels never stand alone, they attach to consonants, changing their sound. They are the marks that modify, the diacritics that shift meaning with a single stroke. Rather than learning Hindi script in isolation, every consonant is anchored to a real English word you already know. A transliteration bridge between two alphabets, two worlds.
