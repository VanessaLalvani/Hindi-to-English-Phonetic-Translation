Independent
Vowels

Independent vowels stand alone, needing no consonant to carry them. They are the sounds that begin, the open syllables that need no anchor. Rather than learning Hindi script in isolation, every vowel is paired with a real English word you already know. A transliteration bridge between two alphabets, two worlds.
